For a more indepth learning experience I am currently enrolling in a series of Udemy courses aimed at giving me a better understanding of these languages fundamentals.

Developing applications is one thing, but developing secure applications that are safe for users to utilize and enjoy is another. I completed the Google Cybersecurity Certificate Program in April, 2024. In May, 2024 I successfully obtained my CompTIA Security+ certfication.
